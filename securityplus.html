Security+
A good certification to get your foot in the door for the world of cybersecurity. This certification will give you knowledge on the terminology and a high level understanding of attacks, architecture and design, implementation, operations and incident response, and governance, risk and compliance. These five level domains all have specializations, but like I said this exam is good to get your foot in the door.
